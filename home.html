Warning Signs

Talking About

  • Feeling unbearable pain
  • Death or recent,fascination with death
  • Feeling hopeless,worthless,trapped
  • Feeling guilt,shame,anger
  • Having no reason to live
  • extreme sadness and fear of perfection
  • Feelings of rejection

Behavioural changes

check whether the person shows these characters

  • Is ashamed to ask for help ,especially help for mental health conditions
  • Calmness after a period of auxicity
  • Losing interest in activities they used to enjoy
  • Withdrawing from friends circle
  • Suddenly having trouble
  • change in sleeping or eating habits
  • Arranging ways to take their own life(such as buying a gun or many pills)
